Science week

Join us for the Fourth Annual Citizen Science Day, a nationally organized event to increase awareness of citizen science and help volunteers discover projects that contribute to the documentation of Earth's Biodiversity. Take action with a wide range of activities, including bird, phenology, and urban naturalist tours, and so much more. April 13th will be national Citizen Science Day Meet with NYBG experts and learn how you can become a Citizen Scientist. April 13th will be national Citizen Science Day
